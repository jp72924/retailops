Module 1
Customers: Your Business Foundation
Every order requires a registered customer. Click each step to explore.
1
Navigate to Customers → New Customer
Click "Customers" in the nav bar, then "New Customer" (top-right button). The registration form opens.
2
Fill in the required fields
First name, last name, and email are required (email must be unique). Phone is optional. Full address including city, state, postal code, and country is required.
3
Add optional notes
Use the Notes field for special instructions, preferred contact methods, or anything your team should know about this customer.
4
Click Save Customer
You're taken to the customer's profile page showing their contact details and full order history. The customer is now available when creating orders.
🔍 Search by name or email
✏️ Edit anytime
🗑️ Delete only if no orders

Inviting a New User
Go to Users → Invite User.
Set name, email, role, and initial password.
User can log in immediately with those credentials.
Ask them to change their password after first login.
Deactivating vs Deleting
Always deactivate, never delete staff who leave.
Deactivated users' historical actions are preserved.
They cannot log in but their audit trail remains.
Reactivate instantly — access restored immediately.
⚙️ Only Admins can see the Users page. You cannot deactivate your own account.
